What it does

A simple loop for seeing clearly.

Clarity gives the mind a place to settle, name what is happening, and loosen what does not need to be held so tightly.

Reflect

Capture a thought, question, or experience by voice or text, then choose whether to keep it local or use Cloud Tap.

Practice

Follow a daily structure that moves from reflection into small practical steps, meditation, wisdom, and compassion.

Loosen

Notice recurring tensions without turning them into fixed identity, diagnosis, or self-judgement.

Buddhist ground

Non-sectarian, practice-first.

Clarity is Buddhist in orientation, but not a replacement for a teacher, lineage, sangha, or tradition. It draws from Tibetan Buddhism, Dzogchen and the Rime spirit, while also making room for Theravada, Zen, and wider Buddhist practice.

Teachings

Public source texts and original reflections point back to impermanence, compassion, ethics, wisdom, and non-grasping.

Texts and songs

Read practice texts, listen to mantra and meditative audio, and keep a simple thread of Dharma in daily life.

Practice tools

Use the meditation timer, calendar observances, daily reflections, wisdom prompts, compassion entries, and progress counters.

Privacy by design

Local first. Cloud only when chosen.

Audio and raw transcripts stay on your iPhone. Cloud Tap only sends selected redacted text when you choose a cloud reflection.

  • No advertising trackers.
  • No selling personal data.
  • No public profile or social feed.
  • Apple handles App Store payments.
