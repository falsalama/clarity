Customer service

Support

For help with Clarity, subscriptions, privacy, or technical issues.

Contact

Email support@clarityapp.uk. Please include your device model, iOS version, app version, and a short description of the issue.

We aim to respond within 2 to 5 working days.

Subscriptions and purchases

Clarity subscriptions and support purchases are handled through Apple's in-app purchase system. You can manage subscriptions in iOS Settings under your Apple Account.

If a purchase is not recognised inside Clarity, open Profile, then Account, then use Restore Purchases.

Privacy or deletion requests

For privacy questions or deletion requests, email support. Do not include sensitive reflection content unless it is necessary for the support request.

Cloud Tap issues

Cloud Tap requires an internet connection, a valid app entitlement, and available backend services. If it fails, check your connection and try again later.

Texts, teachings, audio, and timer

Clarity includes Buddhist learning material, practice texts, mantra and meditative audio, a meditation timer, calendar observances, and daily wisdom or compassion prompts. If a source link, audio track, or practice item looks wrong, email support with the screen name and a short description.

Health permissions

Apple Health access can be changed in iOS Settings. Clarity cannot read Health data unless permission has been granted by you.

Safety

Clarity is not crisis support. If you may be in immediate danger, contact local emergency services or a trusted person now.
