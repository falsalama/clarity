# Clarity Privacy Policy Draft

Last updated: 28 April 2026

This draft is intended to become the public Privacy Policy URL required in App Store Connect. Update the contact details and hosting URL before release.

## Overview

Clarity is a Buddhist-inspired practice app for reflection, meditation, learning, audio, calendar observances, and daily practice support.

Clarity is designed to keep sensitive practice material on your device by default. Cloud features are optional and are used only when you choose them.

## Information Clarity Handles

Clarity may handle:

- Reflection text you write or dictate inside the app.
- On-device transcripts created from your audio, if you use voice capture.
- Local practice progress and daily completion state.
- Local learning and capsule cues used to improve future reflections.
- Apple Health data, if you grant permission, for mindfulness and gentle pattern context.
- An anonymous app account identifier used for Cloud Tap access.
- Purchase entitlement state from Apple, used to unlock paid features.

## On-Device Data

Audio recordings and raw transcripts stay on your iPhone.

Local reflection history, capsule cues, and practice progress are stored on your device unless you explicitly choose a cloud feature.

Apple Health data is used only with your permission. Health data is used for local pattern context and is not sold, used for advertising, or sent to Cloud Tap.

## Cloud Tap

Cloud Tap is optional.

When you choose a Cloud Tap response, Clarity sends selected redacted text and limited context needed to generate the response. Audio files and raw transcripts are not sent.

Cloud Tap is used to provide practice-focused reflection, perspective, options, questions, and talk-it-through support.

## Purchases

Payments are handled by Apple through the App Store.

Clarity uses purchase entitlement state to unlock Clarity Reflect and optional support purchases. Clarity does not receive your full payment card details.

## Account Deletion

Clarity may create an anonymous cloud account to support Cloud Tap access and purchase entitlement checks.

You can delete the current cloud account in the app from Privacy / Cloud Tap → Delete Cloud Account and Data.

Deleting the cloud account removes the current anonymous cloud account and server entitlement record. Local reflections, audio recordings, Health data, and on-device history remain on your iPhone unless you delete the app or clear that data separately.

## Tracking and Advertising

Clarity does not use third-party advertising.

Clarity does not track you across other companies' apps or websites.

Clarity does not sell personal data.

## Location

If you use nearby or pilgrimage-related features, Clarity may request location permission. Location is used to provide app functionality. Do not describe location as collected unless the app later sends it to a server.

## Data Sharing

Clarity shares data only where needed to provide app functionality:

- Apple processes App Store payments and subscriptions.
- Supabase may be used for anonymous account/session functionality and Cloud Tap requests.
- Cloud model providers may process selected redacted text when Cloud Tap is used.

## Scope

Clarity is not medical advice, therapy, diagnosis, treatment, or crisis support.

If you are in immediate danger or need urgent mental health support, contact local emergency services or an appropriate crisis support service.
